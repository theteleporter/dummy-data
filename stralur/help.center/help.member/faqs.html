What is Stralur?

Stralur is a Tv shows and movies streaming platform, that is based on subscriptions that are renewed every month since the moment one becomes a member.

While watching, you can also download the Tv shows and movies on your device, so that you can watch while offline.

Are there kids shows?

Of course, Stralur offers all kinds of streaming categories including kids tv shows and movies. There are plenty of kids tv shows and movies where all the family can enjoy.

Streaming qualities

How does the 10-bit quality differ from normal streaming quality?
Normal quality is supported in all devices whereas, 10-bit is supported by some devices. 10-bit is advantageous, as it takes a low space compared to normal quality, which occupies much space. It is recommended that when downloading, you should consider the quality that is supported by your device.

Subscription plans

While choosing a subscription plan, you should consider well the plan that you are choosing. Stralur charges a subscription every month since the date you signed up.
